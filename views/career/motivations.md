# Motivations

<!-- Used for: "Why do you want this role?", "What drives you?", cover letters -->

## What I care about

- **Leverage**: I'm drawn to work where I can multiply the impact of others — a well-designed platform,
  a clear technical decision, or a good mentoring session can save a team weeks.
- **Craft**: I take pride in building things that are clean, well-tested, and don't rot.
  I find sloppiness in systems frustrating even when it's expedient.
- **Growth**: I want to keep getting better — technically and as a collaborator and communicator.
  I look for environments where I'll be challenged.

## Why I'm looking now

My current role has been a strong learning environment, but I've reached a point where I want
more scope — either broader technical ownership or more direct impact on the product.
I'm looking for a place where the engineering culture matches my standards and there's room to grow
into a staff or lead role.

## What I don't want

- Pure maintenance work with no room to improve or re-design
- Top-down decision making with no engineering input on architecture
- Environments where shipping fast is the only metric
