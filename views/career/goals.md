# Career Goals

<!-- Used for: "Where do you see yourself in X years?", closing interview questions -->

## Short-term (1–2 years)

Deepen my technical leadership skills — owning a meaningful piece of system architecture,
being a reliable voice in engineering decisions, and getting better at communicating
technical trade-offs to non-technical stakeholders.

## Long-term (3–5 years)

Move into a Staff Engineer or Engineering Manager role, depending on how I find the balance
between technical depth and people work. I'm currently more drawn to the staff track —
I want to solve hard technical problems that have organizational impact — but I'm keeping
options open as I learn more about what leadership looks like in practice.

## What success looks like

I'll feel successful if the systems I built are still standing and maintainable 3 years later,
and if engineers I worked with feel like they grew.
