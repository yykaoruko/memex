## Short (30 seconds / ~80 words)

I'm a software engineer with 7 years of experience building backend systems and developer tooling.
I've worked at both startups and mid-size product companies in Tokyo, mostly around API design,
distributed systems, and platform engineering. Recently I've been leading a small team and focusing
on how to make engineering orgs move faster without sacrificing quality.

## Long (2 minutes / ~200 words)

I started my career as a backend engineer, drawn to the challenge of building systems that are
both reliable at scale and maintainable over time. Over the past 7 years I've worked across
different company sizes — from 20-person startups where I touched everything, to 300-person
product companies where I specialized in platform and infrastructure.

A turning point was building an internal developer platform at my current company. What started
as a side project became something 4 teams depend on daily. That experience made me realize
I care deeply not just about the systems I build, but about the people who use them — both
end users and fellow engineers.

Lately I've been growing into a technical lead role: setting architecture direction, mentoring
junior engineers, and working closely with product and design. I'm looking for an environment
where I can go deeper on both the technical and leadership sides.
